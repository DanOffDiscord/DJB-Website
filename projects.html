Projects

These are projects I have worked on or still work on to this day.



Bolty

Bolty is a multi-function Discord bot with a countless amount of features. exchangeFalse (exchangeFalse#4025) is the main developer, and I just help out with a few commands from time to time.
Some of these features could include Urban Dictionary Search, Imgur Upload, and even your own custom error message! We're currently making some really great progress with Bolty, and hopefully it can become a big bot some day.
We are currently working on a rewrite of Bolty, so it will be better than ever.

DJB-Website

DJB-Website is another name for the website you're currently looking at! The simple, clean design was originally designed by myself, but I got an immense amount of help from Lazer, who did a complete resdesign.
I personally really liked the redesign, and is now the main design of the website, huge thanks to Lazer for the redesign.
Later on, I did yet another redesign, which is the current design used at the moment, with a brand new colour scheme and new pages.
If you wish to contribute to this website, feel free to at the repository here.

CharlestonCounty-Website

CharlestonCounty-Website was a website made for a small Roblox roleplaying group a friend, and I was asked to create a cool website for them. I was quite happy with the finished product, however, I still add additions to this day.
If you're interested in the finished product, feel free to visit it here. You can also view the source code, and contribute if you so wish, here.

Lyric

Lyric is a music bot for Discord. As with Bolty, I sometimes help out with commands, and put ideas forward to the main developers, Mason (Mason#5455), Hexabyte (Hexabyte#3169), Ethan (Ethan.#8006), and Anidox (Anidox#6280). We hope Lyric will be a successor to a past project, Astrid, as it will be sharded, meaning faster speeds, and less downtime. Also, I sometimes work on the web dashboard for Lyric, which will come out soon.
